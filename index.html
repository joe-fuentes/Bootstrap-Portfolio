I was born in Jacksonville, Florida. Shortly after, our family moved to the Philippines. I was raised by my mother while in the Philippines. After graduating from high school, I returned to Jacksonville to live with my father. As soon as I landed back here in the United States I started my first job at McDonald's flipping burgers.

I worked as an attractions operator at the Walt Disney World. Then, after I received my Bachelors degree in Electrical Engineering from UCF, I got a position as an engineer, hopping from one company to another - gaining field experience anywhere I can.
